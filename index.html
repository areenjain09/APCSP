The 10 Hottest Places to Visit!

  1. Haleakalā National Park

    Home to Maui's highest peak, this park offers stunning sunrise views and diverse landscapes.

  2. Road to Hāna

    A scenic drive featuring waterfalls, rainforests, and unforgettable coastal views.

  3. Waianapanapa State Park

    Famous for its black sand beach and sea caves, this park is a natural gem.

  4. Molokini Crater

    One of the best snorkeling and diving spots in Maui, with vibrant marine life.

  5. Ka’anapali Beach

    A world-famous beach known for its golden sands and crystal-clear waters.

  6. ʻIao Valley State Monument

    Known for its lush scenery and the iconic ʻIao Needle, this is a must-visit for nature lovers.

  7. Lahaina Historic Town

    A former whaling village, Lahaina is rich in history and has a lively atmosphere.

  8. Maui Ocean Center

    A top-rated aquarium that showcases Hawaii’s unique marine life, including sharks and turtles.

  9. Honolua Bay

    A popular snorkeling spot with pristine coral reefs and abundant marine life.

  10. Mākena State Park (Big Beach)

    This expansive beach offers some of the best sunbathing and bodyboarding on the island.
